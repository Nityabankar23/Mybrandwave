Who We Are

Authentic connection
over paid noise.

MyBrandWave is a content marketing and organic social media agency based in Navi Mumbai — built for brands, businesses, and personal brands that want to grow without being dependent on ad spend.

Kimaya started this at 23 because she kept seeing the same thing: good work going unnoticed online. Not because people didn't care — but because nobody had shown them that content could actually be a strategy, not just a posting schedule.

So that's what we fix. We figure out what your brand sounds like, what your audience actually wants to see, and we build content around that. Reels, carousels, captions — everything designed to make someone stop scrolling and actually give a damn.
